Information We Collect

At Palm Cove Renovations, we collect information to provide better services to our clients. The types of information we collect include:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Project requirements and preferences
  • Budget and timeline information
  • Communication history regarding your renovation project
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)

We collect this information when you submit forms on our website, call us, or interact with our services.

How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us deliver exceptional renovation services:

  • Process and manage your renovation project inquiries
  • Provide accurate quotes and project timelines
  • Communicate project updates and scheduling
  • Send relevant renovation tips and offers (with your consent)
  • Improve our services based on client feedback

We never sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure servers with regular updates
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Regular security audits
