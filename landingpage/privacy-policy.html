Introduction

At Palm Cove Cabinetry, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our custom cabinetry services. We understand that trust is the foundation of our relationship with our clients, and we take our responsibility to protect your information seriously.

By accessing our website or engaging with our services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. We encourage you to read this document carefully to understand our commitment to your privacy.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you request a quote, schedule a consultation, or contact us through our website. This may include:

  • Your name and contact details (email address, phone number)
  • Property address for service delivery
  • Project specifications and preferences
  • Communication history and correspondence
  • Payment information (processed securely through third-party providers)

Additionally, we automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our website, including IP addresses, browser type, device information, and browsing patterns through cookies and similar technologies.

How We Use Your Information

Your information enables us to provide exceptional custom cabinetry services and improve your experience. We use your data to:

  • Process and fulfill your cabinet orders and service requests
  • Communicate about your projects, including quotes and scheduling
  • Send important updates about our services and policies
  • Improve our website functionality and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. Your trust is paramount to our business.

Data Protection & Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our security protocols include:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure servers with regular security updates
  • Limited access to personal information (need-to-know basis)
  • Regular security audits and assessments
  • Compliance with industry-standard security practices

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We continuously work to enhance our security measures.

Your Rights & Choices

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

Access

Request copies of your personal data

Correction

Update or correct inaccurate information

Deletion

Request removal of your personal data

Opt-out

Unsubscribe from marketing communications
